Lessons From The Pros

School Holiday Group Training

Introducing our school holiday masterclasses! Train with some of the most experienced former and current professional players in position specific training to help elevate your game to the next level. Ask any questions you want, learn about what it takes to reach a high level of football and master the intricacies of football that will separate you from the competition.

Masterclasses aren't only locked to your position — if you'd like to learn your trade in another position, by all means jump in on one of our other masterclasses!

Keep updated on upcoming masterclasses via our Instagram and Facebook pages.
