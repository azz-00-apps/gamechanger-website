Testing Protocols

Our testing includes.

Speed Testing

10 and 20m speed testing with sports science speed gate technology. Measures acceleration and overall top speed.

Jump Testing

Complete VALD force plate jump testing to better understand lower body power, stretch shortening cycle and repeat jump ability.

Agility Testing

Complete world recognised agility tests with sports science tec to measure change of direction ability and other crucial factors for football.

Strength Testing

Measure upper & lower body, as well as core strength to assess current physical strength and to further inform S&C program design.
