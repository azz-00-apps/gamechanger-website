## Hero
Heading: **PERFORMANCE TESTING**

> At Gamechanger, we conduct diligent sports science performance testing with state of the art technology. This allows both coach and athlete to understand their physical strengths and weaknesses, create a physical profile, and compare these results with both themselves, and normative data. This testing also informs our Strength and Conditioning programs and allows our high performance coaches to better focus training on the identified areas of weakness. Data drives our training, and all guesswork is removed by ensuring progress is being made, rather than just the eye test.

## Our Testing Includes
Heading: **OUR TESTING INCLUDES:**

### SPEED TESTING
> 10 AND 20M SPEED TESTING WITH SPORTS SCIENCE SPEED GATE TECHNOLOGY. MEASURES ACCELERATION AND OVERALL TOP SPEED.

### JUMP TESTING
> COMPLETE VALD FORCE PLATE JUMP TESTING TO BETTER UNDERSTAND LOWER BODY POWER, STRETCH SHORTENING CYCLE AND REPEAT JUMP ABILITY.

### AGILITY TESTING
> COMPLETE WORLD RECOGNISED AGILITY TESTS WITH SPORTS SCIENCE TEC TO MEASURE CHANGE OF DIRECTION ABILITY AND OTHER CRUCIAL FACTORS FOR Football.

### STRENGTH TESTING
> MEASURE UPPER & LOWER BODY, AS WELL AS CORE STRENGTH TO ASSESS CURRENT PHYSICAL STRENGTH AND TO FURTHER INFORM S&C PROGRAM DESIGN.
