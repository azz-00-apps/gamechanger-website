## Our Method
Heading: **Our Method**
> All athletes upon signing up will have a multitude of discussions with their coach around their playing history, position, goals in football, and their current training loads.

> Training sessions will be completely individualised to each athlete, pending their identified strengths, weaknesses and desired areas of improvement, as well as what the coach discovers throughout weekly training.

> Too many coaches will just set out cones, do gimmicky social media drills, or tell the athlete to dribble, run or pass, this is not game realistic. Our sessions are game realistic, and match position and level specific scenarios that players will face within their competition. Standards will be kept high and intensity will aim to match that of games (pending athlete schedule). These sessions are PROVEN at the highest level to be effective, enjoyable and develop players into the very best that they can be.
