How We Train

Our method.

All athletes upon signing up will have a multitude of discussions with their coach around their playing history, position, goals in football, and their current training loads.

Training sessions will be completely individualised to each athlete, pending their identified strengths, weaknesses and desired areas of improvement, as well as what the coach discovers throughout weekly training.

Too many coaches will just set out cones, do gimmicky social media drills, or tell the athlete to dribble, run or pass — this is not game realistic. Our sessions are game realistic, and match position and level specific scenarios that players will face within their competition. Standards will be kept high and intensity will aim to match that of games (pending athlete schedule). These sessions are proven at the highest level to be effective, enjoyable and develop players into the very best that they can be.

What We Cover

Session focuses.

Sessions cover a wide variety of footballing aspects. These can include:

Technical

Shooting, dribbling, passing, first touch.

Tactical

Body shape and positioning, awareness.

Physical

Reactions, strength, top speed, acceleration, change of direction.

Mental

Advice and guidance on dealing with pressure, performance anxiety, confidence, and more.

The Difference

What makes us different?

At Gamechanger, you will be educated and mentored by coaches who are still playing at the highest of levels in Australian football, and understand what it takes to be a modern day player, dealing with the stresses and pressure that comes with chasing your footballing dreams. Our coaches all regularly train and play at the elite level, and will push you to reach the standards necessary to become an elite player yourself.

We know what its like growing up in the Australian footballing system and not knowing what steps to take and when. We are here to guide you every step of the way, utilising our knowledge and resources from 15+ years of football in the NSW youth footballing space.
